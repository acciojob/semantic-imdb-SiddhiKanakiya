Summary

The movie is about twenty-year-old Will Hunting of South Boston is a natural genius who is self-taught and been recently paroled from jail. Will Hunting works as Janitor at Havard school where he is able to solve difficult problems laid out by professor. So the professor decides to mentor and help him so he can use his full potential. He starts his sessions with Sean- a therapist who unlike others can relate with Will. Will also meets a charming woman named Skylar who studies at Havard in pub. The story tells us how Will fights his inner demons to take over the world
